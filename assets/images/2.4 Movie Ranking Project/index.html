ANNABELLE'S BEST SERIES SO FAR

My top 3 to date!!!

NIGHT AGENT

It's full of suspence, some action scenes, betrayal and love. It will keep you on the edge of your seat all through

THE GLORY

It talks about revenge. Some scenes can be hard to watch. I loved it but had mixed feelings at the end though

THICKER THAN BLOOD

It's about family bonds: it depicts the sacrifices and risks one can take to save or cover up for a loved one
